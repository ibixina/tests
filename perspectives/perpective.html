The Future of Immersive Web

Before the introduction of WebGL, creating true volumetric depth on a web page was impossible. We relied on simple CSS shadows and flat images. Today, we can render complex, ray-traced lighting directly in the browser.

Interact with the frame below. Move your mouse to see the physical depth of the room, then trigger the illusion to break the boundaries of the screen.

Notice how your brain instantly recalculates the position of the object the moment it overlaps the white border. The contrast between the standard text and the volumetric model is what makes the forced perspective successful.
